About Product

The product is an innovative insole system designed to help children with autism who toe-walk develop proper walking habits through gentle sensory feedback. Our system provides vibration when the heel touches the ground, encouraging proper gait development through positive reinforcement.

Currently at the desktop prototype phase and being developed in collaboration with 4D Products, our insoles connect via Bluetooth to this interface, allowing parents and therapists to customize settings and track progress.
